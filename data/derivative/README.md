- [**tmQM-RDF**](/data/derivative/tmQM-RDF). The tmQM-RDF dataset: a knowledge graph on TMCs that synthesises the information coming from the following datasets: [tmQM](https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQM) (Balcells and Skjelstad, 2020), [tmQMg](https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQMg) (Kneiding et al, 2023), [tmQMg-L](https://github.com/hkneiding/tmQMg-L/tree/main?tab=readme-ov-file) (Kneiding et al, 2024).
- [**tmQM-RDF-1Ksel**](/data/derivative/tmQM-RDF-1Ksel). The 1K selections of tmQM-RDF: two selections containing 1000 training TMCs (plus 300 validation and 300 test TMCs), one focusing on early transition metals (Cr, Mo, W, tag `earlyTM`) and another one focusing on late transition metal (Pd, Ni, Pt, tag `lateTM`).
