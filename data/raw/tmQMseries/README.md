This directory contains the original data for the tmQM-RDF series: tmQM (Balcells and Skjelstad, 2020), tmQMg (Kneiding et al., 2023) tmQMg-L (Kneiding et al., 2024).
The script [download_data.py](/data/raw/tmQMseries/download_data.py) automaticlly downloads and unpacks the required resources from the following locations:
- tmQM:
  - 2024 release: [https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQM/](https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQM/)
- tmQMg:
  - v74k (September 2024 release): [https://ns9999k.webs.sigma2.no/10.11582_2024.00139/nird/home/hanneskn/tmQMg/](https://data.archive.sigma2.no/dataset/cc354a73-7398-487f-83f8-4166caa8cc09/download/nird/home/hanneskn/tmQMg/uNatQ_graphs.zip)
  - v74k (November 2025 update): [https://data.archive.sigma2.no/dataset/4f94f626-b18c-458c-946f-d7052bb05982/download/u-NatQ_graphs.zip](https://data.archive.sigma2.no/dataset/4f94f626-b18c-458c-946f-d7052bb05982/download/u-NatQ_graphs.zip)
- tmQMg-L:
  - v60k: [https://github.com/hkneiding/tmQMg-L/archive/refs/tags/v60k.zip](https://github.com/hkneiding/tmQMg-L/archive/refs/tags/v60k.zip)
  - v74k: [https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQMg-L/archive/refs/tags/v74k.zip](https://github.com/uiocompcat/tmQMg-L/archive/refs/tags/v74k.zip)

See the [tmQM-RDF release history](/README.md) for more information on how and which versions are used.

Kneiding, Hannes, Ruslan Lukin, Lucas Lang, et al. 2023. “Deep Learning Metal Complex Properties with Natural Quantum Graphs.” Digital Discovery 2 (3): 618–33. https://doi.org/10.1039/D2DD00129B.

Kneiding, Hannes, Ainara Nova, and David Balcells. 2024. “Directional Multiobjective Optimization of Metal Complexes at the Billion-System Scale.” Nature Computational Science 4 (4): 263–73. https://doi.org/10.1038/s43588-024-00616-5.
